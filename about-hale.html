Mission


Our mission is to revolutionize the management of medical records and appointments at Ateneo de Naga University’s AHEHSO through HALE, the AdNU-Based Healthcare and Lifeline Environment, for all the bonafide members of the institution. HALE aims to provide seamless electronic record storage, efficient appointment scheduling, and secure access to medical services, all while supporting ongoing healthcare system research to ensure the highest quality of care.


Vision


Our vision is to create a fully digitized, streamlined healthcare management system that enhances the efficiency and effectiveness of medical services at Ateneo de Naga University’s AHEHSO. Through innovation and continuous improvement, we aspire to set a new standard for healthcare management, contributing to a healthier and more informed institution.
